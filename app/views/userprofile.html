Hello, fellow baking enthusiasts! I'm Buddy Valastro, but you might know me better as the Cake Boss. Born into the world of baking, with the rich legacy of Carlo's Bakery in my veins, I've been immersed in the art of cake making since I could barely see over the counter. My journey has been one of passion, creativity, and a relentless pursuit of baking perfection.

At the heart of Hoboken, New Jersey, I've taken the reins of our family-run bakery, turning it into a household name and a destination for cake lovers from around the globe. My mission is simple: to bring a sprinkle of joy and a dollop of delight into every cake I create, blending traditional techniques with innovative designs that push the boundaries of edible art.

On this platform, I'm excited to share with you a slice of my world. You'll find recipes that have been refined through generations, alongside modern creations that have dazzled on television and at grand events. From sumptuous wedding cakes that tower to the heavens to whimsical cupcakes that bring out the child in all of us, there's a recipe here for every occasion and skill level.
